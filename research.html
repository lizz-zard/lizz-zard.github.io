This summer I'm working as an undergraduate researcher assistant looking into zero-knowledge proofs under Professor Jeremy Clark. Together with Professor Clark and Masters student Youwei Deng, I am writing a handbook for gadgets in the polynomial interactive oracle proof (Poly-IOP) model used by the succinct non-interactive argument of knowledge (SNARK) Plonk. I also looking at the applications of Plonk-style poly-IOPs for proof of solvency as well as implementing call markets.
